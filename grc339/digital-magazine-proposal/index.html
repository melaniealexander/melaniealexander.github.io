Introduction

Forma Viva

Forma Viva honors the artistry, discipline, and elegance that is the sport of rhythmic gymnastics. Rhythmic gymnastics explores the balance between athleticism and expressive movement, and this digital magazine celebrates the individuals and teams who elevate the sport into the Olympic caliber it is at today. The beauty of rhythmic gymnastics is not just performance. It’s sport in the most refined form.
